Good morning, and welcome to Oniton Hall, one of the largest estates in the area. 大家早上好，欢迎来到 Oniton Hall，这里是本地区最大的庄园之一。⭐ estate = （大型）庄园、地产（Describe a place 高频）；one of the largest… = 最……之一（万能句型）
My name's Nick, and I'm one of the guides. 我叫 Nick，是这里的一名导游。
I'll give you a brief introduction to the estate while you're sitting down, and then we'll walk round. 趁大家还坐着，我先简单介绍一下这座庄园，之后我们再一起参观。⭐ give a brief introduction to… = 简单介绍……；walk round = 四处参观（英式英语）
The estate consists of the house, gardens, parkland and farm, and it dates back to the fourteenth century. 这座庄园包括主宅、花园、园林和农场，它的历史可以追溯到14世纪。⭐ consist of… = 由……组成；date back to… = 追溯到……（雅思高频）
The original house was replaced in the late seventeenth century, and of course it has had a large number of owners. 最初的房子在17世纪晚期被重建了，当然，这座庄园也经历过许多任主人。⭐ the original… = 最初的……；a large number of… = 很多……
Almost all of them have left their mark, generally by adding new rooms, like the ballroom and conservatory, or by demolishing others. 几乎每一位主人都留下了自己的印记，通常是增建新的房间，比如舞厅和温室，或者拆掉原有的一些房间。⭐ leave one's mark = 留下自己的印记（⭐⭐⭐推荐）；demolish = 拆除；conservatory = 温室、玻璃花房
The farm looks much as it's always done, although the current owner has done a great deal of work to the flower beds. 农场基本保持着过去的样子，不过现任主人在花坛上下了不少功夫。⭐ do a great deal of work to/on… = 在……上投入大量精力；flower beds = 花坛
In the seventeenth century, the estate was owned by a very wealthy man called Sir Edward Downes. 17世纪时，这座庄园属于一位非常富有的人，名叫 Sir Edward Downes。⭐ be owned by… = 归……所有；wealthy = 富有的（比 rich 更正式）
His intention was to escape from the world of politics, after years as an active politician, and to build a new house worthy of his big collection of books, paintings and sculptures. 他希望远离政坛，在多年从政之后，建造一座足以收藏自己大量书籍、绘画和雕塑的新宅。⭐ His intention was to… = 他的目的是……；escape from… = 远离……；be worthy of… = 配得上……；足以容纳……；a collection of… = 一系列收藏
He broke off contact with his former political allies, and hosted meetings of creative and literary people, like painters and poets. 他与过去的政治盟友断绝了联系，转而邀请画家、诗人等富有创造力和文学气息的人来这里聚会。⭐ break off contact with… = 与……断绝联系（⭐⭐⭐）；former = 前任的；过去的；host meetings = 举办聚会/会议；literary = 文学的
Unusually for his time, he didn't care whether his guests were rich or poor, as long as they had talent. 在当时那个年代，这一点很特别：他并不在乎客人是富有还是贫穷，只要他们有才华就行。⭐ Unusually for his time = 在那个年代，这一点很不寻常（⭐⭐⭐）；as long as… = 只要……
Big houses like Oniton had dozens of servants until the 1920s or 30s, and we've tried to show what their working lives were like. 像 Oniton 这样的大庄园一直到20世纪二三十年代都还有几十位仆人，我们也尽力还原了他们当时的工作生活是什么样的。⭐ dozens of… = 数十个……；working life = 工作生活（可迁移到 Describe a job）
Photographs of course don't give much of an idea, so instead, as you go round the house, you'll see volunteers dressed up as nineteenth-century servants, going about their work. 当然，光靠照片很难让人真正了解当时的情况，所以当你参观这座宅子时，你会看到志愿者穿着19世纪仆人的服装，现场演示他们当时的工作。⭐ give an idea of… = 让人了解……；dress up as… = 打扮成……；go about one's work = 忙着自己的工作；干活（⭐⭐⭐很自然）
They'll explain what they're doing, and tell you their recipes, or what tools they're using. 他们会一边演示自己在做什么，一边介绍当时使用的食谱或工具。⭐ recipe = 食谱；tool = 工具
We've just introduced this feature to replace the audio guide we used to have available. 这是我们刚推出的新项目，用来取代以前提供的语音导览。⭐ introduce a feature = 推出一项新功能/新项目；replace… = 取代……；audio guide = 语音导览
I see there are a number of children here with you today. 我看到今天有不少孩子也跟着一起来了。⭐ a number of… = 不少；很多
Well, we have several activities specially for children, like dressing up in the sorts of clothes that children wore in the past, and as it's a fine day, some of you will probably want to play in the adventure playground. 我们专门为孩子们准备了不少活动，比如穿上传统服饰，体验过去孩子们穿的衣服。如果今天天气不错的话，你们当中有些人应该也会想去探险游乐场玩一玩。⭐ be specially for… = 专门为……准备；dress up in… = 穿上……；adventure playground = 探险游乐场；a fine day = 天气很好（英式表达）
Our latest addition is child-sized tractors, that you can drive around the grounds. 我们最新增加的项目是儿童尺寸的小拖拉机，孩子们可以开着它在庄园里转一圈。⭐ latest addition = 最新增设的项目（⭐⭐⭐）；drive around… = 开着……四处转；grounds = （庄园、学校等的）场地、园区
We'll also be going into the farm that's part of the estate, where there's plenty to do. 接下来我们还会去庄园里的农场，那里有很多可以体验的活动。⭐ there's plenty to do = 有很多事情可以做（口语高频⭐⭐⭐）
Most of the buildings date from the eighteenth century, so you can really step back into an agricultural past. 农场里的大多数建筑都建于18世纪，所以你会有一种真正穿越回过去乡村生活的感觉。⭐ date from… = 始建于……（≈ date back to）；step back into… = 仿佛回到……（⭐⭐⭐）；agricultural = 农业的；乡村农耕的
Until recently, the dairy was where milk from the cows was turned into cheese. 直到不久前，这间奶制品加工房还是把牛奶制作成奶酪的地方。⭐ dairy = 奶制品加工房；乳品厂；turn…into… = 把……变成……
It's now the place to go for lunch, or afternoon tea, or just a cup of coffee and a slice of homemade cake. 现在这里已经改成了餐厅，大家可以来这里吃午餐、喝下午茶，或者点一杯咖啡配一块自制蛋糕。⭐ the place to go = ……的首选地点（⭐⭐⭐非常地道）；a slice of… = 一片/一块……
The big stone building that dominates the farm is the large barn, and in here is our collection of agricultural tools. 农场里最显眼的那栋石头建筑就是大谷仓，里面陈列着我们收藏的各种传统农具。⭐ dominate = 占据主导位置；最显眼；barn = 谷仓；collection of… = 一系列收藏
These were used in the past to plough the earth, sow seeds, make gates, and much more. 这些工具过去主要用来翻土、播种、制作栅栏门等等。⭐ plough the earth = 翻土；sow seeds = 播种（雅思高频）
There's a small barn, also made of stone, where you can groom the donkeys and horses, to keep their coats clean. 还有一间同样由石头建成的小谷仓，在那里你可以帮驴子和马梳理毛发，让它们保持干净。⭐ groom = 给（马、狗等动物）梳毛、护理（⭐⭐⭐）；coat = （动物的）皮毛
They really seem to enjoy having it done, and children love grooming them. 它们看起来真的很享受被梳毛，而孩子们也特别喜欢帮它们打理毛发。⭐ seem to enjoy doing… = 看起来很享受……；have something done = 让别人为自己做某事；这里表示"被梳毛"
The horses no longer live in the stables, which instead is the place to go to buy gifts, books, our own jams and pickles, and clothes and blankets made of wool from our sheep. 这些马现在已经不住在马厩里了。如今，马厩被改造成了一家商店，在那里可以买到礼品、书籍、自制果酱、腌菜，以及用农场羊毛制作的衣物和毛毯。⭐ stable = 马厩；the place to go = 首选地点（高频）；made of… = 由……制成
Outside the shed, which is the only brick building, you can climb into a horse-drawn carriage for a lovely, relaxing tour of the park and farm. 谷仓外面有一栋唯一的砖房，在那里你可以登上一辆马车，悠闲地游览整个庄园和农场。⭐ shed = 棚屋；仓库；brick building = 砖房；horse-drawn carriage = 马车（⭐⭐⭐）；climb into… = 登上（车辆）
The carriages are well over a hundred years old. 这些马车都有一百多年的历史了。⭐ well over… = 远远超过……
And finally, the parkland, which was laid out in the eighteenth century, with a lake and trees that are now well established. 最后，我们会来到庄园的园林。这片园林始建于18世纪，有一座湖泊，还有如今已经长得十分茂盛的大树。⭐ lay out = 规划、设计（园林、城市等）；well established = 生长成熟的；根基稳固的
You'll see types of cattle and sheep that are hardly ever found on farms these days. 你们还会看到一些如今在普通农场几乎已经见不到的牛羊品种。⭐ hardly ever = 几乎从不；几乎没有（口语高频）；be found = 能够见到
We're helping to preserve them, to stop their numbers falling further. 我们一直在努力保护这些品种，防止它们的数量继续减少。⭐ preserve = 保护；保存（⭐⭐⭐）；stop…from doing… = 阻止……；fall further = 进一步下降
OK, well if you'd like to come with me … 好了，如果大家准备好了，就请跟我一起出发吧。⭐ come with me = 跟我来（导览常用结束语）
